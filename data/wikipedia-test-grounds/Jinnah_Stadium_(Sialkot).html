Jinnah Stadium, one of the oldest cricket grounds in Pakistan, is located in Sialkot. Before independence, it was called Connelly Park. After independence, it was renamed as Jinnah Park. The reconstruction of Jinnah Stadium was started in 1979, and was completed in 1984. It is currently used mostly for cricket matches. The stadium holds 30,000 and hosted its first Test match in 1985.[1] It is the home ground of Sialkot Stallions.

Test[edit]

  • Lowest Team Total: Sri Lanka 157 v Pakistan 27 Oct 1985
  • Highest Individual Score:Moin Khan Pakistan vs Sri Lanka 22 Sep 1995

One Day International[edit]
