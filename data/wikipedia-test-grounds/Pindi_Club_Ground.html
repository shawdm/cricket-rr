Pindi Club Ground
Location Rawalpindi, Punjab, Pakistan
Owner Pakistan Cricket Board
Capacity 15,000
Surface Grass

Pindi Club Ground is a multi-use stadium in Rawalpindi, Pakistan. It is used mostly for cricket matches. The stadium has the capacity to hold 15,000 people and hosted its first and only Test Match in 1965.[1]
