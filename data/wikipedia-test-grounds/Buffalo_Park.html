Buffalo Park is a cricket ground located in East London, Eastern Cape, South Africa. It is one of the home grounds for the Warriors cricket team. It can hold up to 20,000 spectators.

It has hosted one Test match, in October 2002 and hosted a number of One Day Internationals.

It was once also known as Mercedes-Benz Park, thanks to sponsorship from the German motor manufacturer.
